What is sepsis?

Sepsis is the bodys overwhelming and life-threatening response to infection that can lead to tissue damage, organ failure, and death. Medically, sepsis is your bodys immune system overresponding to an infection.

Spot the symptoms

For all media enquiries, please email press@sepsistrust.org or call 0121 716 5150

What we do

Although treatable in many cases, at least 48,000 deaths a year in the UK are related to sepsis. Were here to save lives and improve outcomes for survivors. We raise public awareness, educate health professionals and support those affected by sepsis.
